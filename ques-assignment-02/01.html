Time is a Paradox Itself

As a definition says, there was no doubt that time is the indefinite continued progress of existence & extinction. Time has been present since everything thing came into existence and it remains till they ended up.There are so many philosophies, perceptions, definitions & measurements of the time.

According to Carlo Rovelli, the time is an illusion. Our naive perception of its flow doesn't correspond to physical reality. The concept of time is simply an illusion made-up of human memories.
